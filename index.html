About Me

Hi, I’m Hems, a computer science engineer from Coimbatore with a passion for photography and digital marketing. I’ve worked with the TeNet group as a Project Associate and Infosys as a Programmer Analyst. After taking a break to care for my family, I moved to the US, traveled to over 20 national parks (planning all the trips), completed a Data Science specialization on Coursera, and coached a Lego League Junior team called Hercules that won the Rising Star award. Back in India since 2018, I’ve supported my family through medical care and earned Google certifications in Digital Marketing and Project Management. I’m excited to help small businesses grow their digital presence and achieve success.
